HOW IT WORKS — FREEZER TO TABLE
Step 1 — Take from Freezer
Remove & Thaw
Remove packet from deep freezer. For chapatis, place on a dry cloth briefly. For puris and bhatura, fry directly — no thawing needed.
No skill required
Step 2 — Bake or Fry
Heat & Cook
Chapati: 60 seconds on a hot tawa. Puri & Bhatura: 10–15 seconds in hot oil at 180°C. Perfectly puffed, golden and consistent every time.
60 seconds · Any team member can do it
Step 3 — Serve Hot & Fresh
Serve & Store
Soft, aromatic, fresh-tasting. Cook only what you need and put the rest back in the freezer. Zero wastage, zero over-prep, zero compromise.
Zero wastage · Store back in freezer
